Frequently Asked Questions

What is an OTC Trading

Over-the-counter (OTC) trading allows two parties to trade with one another, agreeing a price privately and to fulfil large orders often not possible using an exchange.

Why use an OTC Desk and not an Exchange

Exchange related risks are minimised when using an experienced OTC desk, particularly when trading large volumes, liquidity can be fragmented on Exchanges. Exchange prices can be subject to slippage and true market depth can be opaque. Prices on an OTC desk can be agreed between two parties in advance of the trade being executed

Why Do OTC Desks Exist

OTC desks cater to institutions and high net worth individuals looking to execute high volume transactions that would not be suitable for exchanges. OTC desks offer a tailored and bespoke service to the customers needs.
